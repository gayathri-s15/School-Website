CAMPUS LIFE OF STEPPING STONE

HOSTEL

Home Away From Home

The students who are staying in the hostel. We provide well-stocked fresh and nutritious food that caters to varied taste. The rooms are pleasant, hygienic and kept neat and clean by the housekeeping staff. The bathrooms and toilets are well designed and adequate. Running hot water round the clock is also available. The School provides separate air-conditioned accommodation for boys and girls. The rooms are furnished artistically to provide comfort and perfect environment to study.

SPACE TO PLAY

Motto of "The stepping stone"

The social, physical and emotional growth of every child under our care. The school offers sports as a compulsory part co-scholastic activity, which includes Cricket, Basketball, Archery, Volleyball, Football, Tennis, Athletics, Judo, Karate, Table Tennis, Badminton, Handball, Horse Riding, Chess, Yoga and indigenous sports like Kabaddi and Kho- Kho. The school has good infrastructure and separate grounds for different sports, including a five-layered synthetic basketball & tennis courts. Students are motivated to participate in all kinds of indoor and outdoor games at the state and national levels.

Impressive literature and prose

DINNING HALL

Unique and special foods

It is clean and spacious to seat all the students and staff together. It is where the children learn table manners and table etiquette. We keep our menu seasonal well balanced and wholesome; maintaining the School Nutritional Guidelines.We include an array of recipes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, school time munchies, after school treats, sweets and refreshing drinks.

TRANSPORT

Transport Scheduling

The transport management system allows the school the schedule the various school routes in an efficient manner. It also allows for the school administrative team to easily delete routes, assign new routes for students, and assign bus supervisors to routes. These tasks when done by a human administrator may lead to errors, plus it can be a time and energy-consuming task. However, when delegated to the transport management system like Fedena, this task is taken care of in an efficient and error-free manner.
